# Privacy & Safety Principles

<Banner>
Clannect protects its users by prioritizing privacy, security, and responsible data practices.
</Banner>

---

## User Privacy

Privacy is a fundamental right.

- Only collect data necessary to operate and improve the platform  
- Be transparent about what is collected and why  
- Allow users control over their personal information  
- Do not share or sell personal data without explicit consent  

Respecting privacy builds trust and accountability.

---

## Data Security

Security is a shared responsibility.

- Implement technical safeguards to protect user data  
- Monitor and respond to potential breaches proactively  
- Encourage strong passwords and account protection practices  
- Ensure backups and recovery plans are in place  

Users should feel confident that their data is safe on Clannect.

---

## Safe Interactions

Safety is embedded in every interaction.

- Prevent harassment, abuse, and harmful behavior  
- Provide clear reporting tools and responsive support  
- Educate users on responsible platform use  
- Follow moderation guidelines consistently  

A safe environment promotes participation and trust.

---

## Responsible Data Use

Data should be used to improve the platform, not manipulate users.

- Metrics inform design, not exploit behavior  
- Analytics are aggregated and anonymized when possible  
- Avoid using sensitive data to influence engagement in unfair ways  

Transparency in data use reinforces ethical design.

---

## Compliance & Legal Standards

Clannect aligns with international privacy and safety regulations.

- GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable laws are followed  
- User rights to access, correct, or delete data are respected  
- Platform policies are updated regularly to reflect legal obligations  

Compliance ensures global trust and credibility.
