## What is Clannect?

Clannect combines social interaction with structured engagement mechanics.

Instead of passive scrolling, users actively participate through posting, conversations, performance tracking, and network building.

Your activity shapes your profile, your reach, and your influence.

---

## Core Concepts

<Accordion title="Profile and Identity">

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Engagement">
